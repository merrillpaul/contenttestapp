Administration

A delay interval of approximately 30 minutes should occur between Trials VI and VII.

You may praise participants as they recall more words.

Scoring

Correct (C): The first recall of a correct word from the target list.

Repetition (R): Any response (e.g., target word or intrusion) that is repeated on the same trial.

Non-Category Intrusion (NI): A word that does not belong to any of the categories.

Synonym Intrusion (SI): Category Intrusion that is a synonym of a target word. 

Across-List Intrusion (AI): A non-target word that belongs to one of the categories to which the child has been exposed (e.g., words from List A when asked to read List B)
