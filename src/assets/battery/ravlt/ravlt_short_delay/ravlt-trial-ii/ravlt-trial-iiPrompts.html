When the examinee indicates that s/he can recall no more words, say, Now I’m going to read the same list again, and once again when I stop, I want you to tell me as many words as you can remember, including words you said the first time.  It doesn’t matter in what order you say them, just say as many words as you can remember, whether or not you said them before.

After you have finished reading the list of words, touch the iPad’s screen and swipe left to start recording the examinee’s responses.
