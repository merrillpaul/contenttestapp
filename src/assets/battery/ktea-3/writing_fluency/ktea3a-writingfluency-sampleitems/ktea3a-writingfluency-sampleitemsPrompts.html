Introduce the subtest by saying, Now I’ll ask you to do some writing.

Point to the first sample item picture and say, This picture shows a girl running. Point to the sentence and say, A good, short sentence that tells what’s happening in this picture is...A girl is running. This sentence tells who is in the picture and what she’s doing.

If the examinee asks if other responses are correct, you may offer instructional feedback. Other examples of acceptable responses include She runs and The person is walking.

Point to the second sample item picture and say, Here is another picture. A good, short sentence that tells what’s happening in this picture is...The pig sleeps. This sentence tells who is in the picture and what it’s doing.

If the examinee asks if other responses are correct, you may offer instructional feedback. Other examples of acceptable responses include It is sleeping and A pig is lying down.
