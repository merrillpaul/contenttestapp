Open the Response Booklet to the Math Fluency pages and place it in front of the examinee. Give the examinee a pencil.

When I say Begin, I want you to work as many of these math problems as you can in 1 minute. Start here (point to the first item) and work the problems in this order (demonstrate by sweeping your finger from left to right across each row down the first page, then across each row down the next page). Keep going until I tell you to stop or you see the stop sign (point to the stop sign). If you don’t know an answer, just skip that one. If you make a mistake, just cross it out. Do not use your eraser. Work as fast as you can, but be sure to watch the signs.

Do you have any questions? (Pause for response.) Teach further as needed, and then say, Begin. Start timing.

After exactly 1 minute (60 seconds), say, Stop.
