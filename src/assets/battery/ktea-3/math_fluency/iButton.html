Scoring

Score 0 or 1 point.

  • Do not penalize for reversal or numeral formation errors.
  • Transposition errors, such as writing 61 for 16, are penalized.

Use the Math Fluency Scoring Key to verify correct answers. The scoring key is stored in Q-i Central under the Support tab (select Test Materials).
