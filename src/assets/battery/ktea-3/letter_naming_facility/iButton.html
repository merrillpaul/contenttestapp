Materials 

Client Device (Raised)

Administration

Start
Grades K–12+: Practice Items, then Trial 1

Discontinue
If one or more uncorrected errors are made during the practice items.

Timing

For each trial, begin timing after saying the last word of instructions. Stop timing when the examinee completes all of the letters for that trial.

Scoring

Each letter is worth 1 point. Any letter not marked as an Uncorrected Error will be counted as correct. The raw score is based on the total time and total errors for both trials.

Do not count self-corrections or repetitions as errors.

Uncorrected Error: Occurs when the examinee provides an incorrect response or skips a letter, and does not correct the incorrect or skipped response. 

Self-correction: Occurs when the examinee provides an incorrect response or skips a letter, but corrects the incorrect or skipped response.

Refer to the KTEA–3 Administration Manual for additional information.
