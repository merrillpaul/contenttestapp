Materials

Client Device (Raised)
Paper and pencil starting at Item 36

Administration

Start
Grade PK: Item 1
Grade K: Item 7
Grade 1: Item 11
Grade 2: Item 18
Grade 3: Item 23
Grades 4–5: Item 28
Grades 6–12+: Item 39

Reverse
Grades K–12+: Must pass the first 3 items administered, or drop back one start point. Repeat as needed.

Discontinue
After 4 consecutive scores of 0.

General Instructions
All word problems are read aloud by the examiner.

Recording

Don’t Know (DK): Used to record when the examinee provides an answer of “I don’t know” as his or her only answer.

Self-Correct (SC): Used to record when the examinee self-corrects.

Repeated Item (R): Used to record when the examinee asks for an item to be repeated or when it appears that he or she was not attending.

Prompt (P): Used to record when an item-specific prompt is provided.

Scoring

All items: Score 0 or 1 point.
  • Responses need not be reduced to lowest terms unless specified.
  • Improper fractions are acceptable.
  • In general, any answer that is numerically equivalent to the correct answer is correct.
  • If the correct answer includes information in parentheses, that information is not required. On most items, there is no penalty for omitting the unit of measurement.

Refer to the KTEA–3 Administration and Scoring Manuals for additional information.
