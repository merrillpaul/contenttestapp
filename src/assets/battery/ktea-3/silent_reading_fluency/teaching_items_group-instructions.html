Point to the teaching items and say, When I say Begin, read these to yourself and answer each question by marking Yes or No. Do them as fast as you can without making mistakes. If you make a mistake, just cross it out. Do not use your eraser. Begin.

If the examinee answers all teaching items correctly, proceed to test items.

If the examinee answers 3 or more of the teaching items incorrectly, discontinue administration.

If the examinee responds incorrectly to 1 or 2 of the teaching items, ask the examinee to read the item(s) aloud. Correct any reading errors and teach further as needed to ensure the examinee understands what he or she is being asked to do.

For each incorrect response, prompt the examinee to correct the response by crossing out the incorrect response and marking the correct answer.

Do you have any questions? (Pause for response.) Teach further as needed, and then proceed to the
test items.
