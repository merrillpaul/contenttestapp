I want you to read some questions and answer each one yes or no. We’ll do the first two together. Proceed to the sample items.

Sample A

Point to the first sample item. Say, Is water dry? Yes or no? (Pause for response.) Water is not dry, so mark No like this (draw a slash mark on top of the dotted line through the word No).

Sample B

Point to the next sample item and say, The next one asks Are some apples red? Yes or no? (Pause for response.) Some apples are red, so mark Yes (encourage the examinee to draw a slash mark through the word Yes). If you were to make a mistake and mark No (draw a slash mark through the word No), you don’t need to erase it. Just cross it out like this (draw another slash mark to make an X through the word No) and then mark the correct answer.

Do you have any questions? (Pause for response.) Teach further as needed, and then proceed to the
teaching items.
