Materials

Response Booklet, Form A
Pencil

Administration

Start
Grades 1–12+: Sample A

Discontinue
If the examinee answers 3 or more of the teaching items incorrectly, discontinue administration.

Timing

Allow exactly 2 minutes (120 seconds) to complete as many items as possible.

Recording

Don’t Know (DK): Used to record when the examinee provides an answer of “I don’t know” as his or her only answer.

No Response (NR): Used to record when the examinee does not respond.

Self-Correct (SC): Used to record when the examinee self-corrects.

Scoring

Enter the item responses for automated scoring. For items skipped or not attempted, select NR. The raw score is the number of correct responses minus the number of incorrect responses.

Refer to the KTEA–3 Administration Manual for additional information.
