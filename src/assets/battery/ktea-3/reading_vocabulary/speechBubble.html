Instructions
Examinee asks for a word from a test item to be read aloud
Say, I can’t read the words for you.
Examinee chooses more than one word
Say, You can only choose one word. Score the examinee’s final response.
Examinee points to his or her response choice rather than reading it aloud
Allow the examinee to respond by pointing and score the response.
Examinee asks whether to read silently or aloud
Say, Either way is fine.
Examinee mispronounces word(s) while reading aloud
Do not penalize for any mispronunciations.
Examinee appears to be responding without reading
Remind him or her to read the words/sentences.
