Materials

Client Device (Raised)

Administration

Start
Grades 1–3: Item 1
Grades 4–12+: Samples A & B, then Item 13

Reverse
Grades 4–12+: Must pass the first 3 items administered, or drop back to Item 1.

Discontinue
After 4 consecutive scores of 0.

Recording

Don’t Know (DK): Used to record when the examinee provides an answer of “I don’t know” as his or her only answer.

No Response (NR): Used to record when the examinee does not respond.

Self-Correct (SC): Used to record when the examinee self-corrects.

Scoring

Score 0 or 1 point.

Refer to the KTEA–3 Administration Manual for additional information.
