Materials

Client Device (Raised)

Administration

Prior to administration, listen to the audio file for Nonsense Word Decoding (reference use only). Audio files are stored in Q-i Central under the Support tab (select Test Materials).

Start
Grades 1–12+: Item 1

Discontinue
After 4 consecutive scores of 0. Allow the examinee to complete remaining items on the client device before discontinuing. Credit is not awarded for items beyond the discontinue point.

Recording

Don’t Know (DK): Used to record when the examinee provides an answer of “I don’t know” as his or her only answer.

No Response (NR): Used to record when the examinee does not respond.

Error Analysis: Used to record incorrect responses verbatim and categorize errors by type.

Scoring

Score 0 or 1 point. 

  • Correct pronunciations are shown on the screen and may be heard on the audio file. Some nonsense words have more than one acceptable pronunciation. The syllable breaks should not be applied rigidly.
  • For credit, the examinee must say the nonsense word as a connected, relatively smooth whole. There is no penalty for earlier partial or disconnected pronunciations as long as the final, smooth pronunciation is correct.
  • Do not penalize for articulation differences due to dialect, regional speech patterns, or a first language other than English (e.g., an examinee whose first language is Spanish may pronounce the letter v as /b/).
  • Do not penalize for misplacement of the accent (i.e., accenting the wrong syllable[s]).
  • Do not penalize for the American dialectal variation of pronouncing /t/ as /d/, such as saying NUR dul for knurtle or NOW dee ist for pnoutiest.

Refer to the KTEA–3 Administration and Scoring Manuals for additional information.
