Client Device (Flat)

Administration

Start
Grades PK-K: Item 1
Grade 1: Item 17
Grade 2: Item 22
Grade 3: Item 28
Grades 4-7: Item 40
Grades 8-12+: Item 52

Reverse
Grades 1-12+: Must pass first 3 items administered or drop back to Item 1.

Discontinue
After 4 consecutive scores of 0. Allow the examinee to complete remaining items on the examinee screen before discontinuing, but do not award credit for items beyond the discontinue point.

Recording

Don’t Know (DK): Used to record when the examinee provides an answer of “I don’t know” as his or her only answer.

No Response (NR): Used to record when the examinee does not respond.

Scoring

Score 0 or 1 point.

  • Some words have more than one acceptable pronunciation. The syllable breaks should not be applied rigidly.

  • For credit, the examinee must say the word as a connected, relatively smooth whole, with the accent on the proper syllable. There is no penalty for earlier partial or disconnected pronunciations as long as the final, smooth pronunciation is correct.

  • Do not penalize for articulation differences due to dialect, regional speech patterns, or a first language other than English (e.g., an examinee whose first language is Spanish may pronounce the letter v as /b/).

  • Do not penalize for pronouncing tu as tyoo instead of too (e.g., tumultuous may be pronounced as tyoo MUHL tyoo us).

  • Do not penalize for the American dialectal variation of pronouncing /t/ as /d/ in certain words, such as saying yoo NYE did for united or pen TAM uh dur for pentameter.

Refer to the KTEA-3 Administration Manual for additional information.
