Instructions
Examinee asks how to spell a word
Spelling is not scored in the Written Expression subtest. Say, Spelling doesn’t count. Just try your best.
Examinee requests a repetition
Repeat story segments or item instructions as necessary.
Examinee writes responses in the wrong place, or asks where she or he should write her or his response
Direct the examinee to write the response next to the corresponding item number.
Examinee's response is incomprehensible
Score 0 for all criteria, except for capitalization and punctuation, which might still be scorable.
LEVELS 2–4
Examinee asks to look back through story
The examinee may refer to earlier WE Booklet pages as needed during the essay writing item.
