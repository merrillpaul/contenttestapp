Instructions
Examinee loses his or her place or skips a word
Redirect him or her by pointing to the appropriate place on the screen.
Examinee becomes stuck on an item
Say, Try the next one.
Examinee says the word in disconnected pieces and then stops
Say, Now say it altogether.
If the examinee responds too quickly to a stimulus word for you to record the incorrect response
Do not ask the examinee to repeat the response. Instead, ask the examinee to respond more slowly on subsequent items. Use the audio recording to verify scoring.
