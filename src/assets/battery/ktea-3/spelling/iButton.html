Materials

Response Booklet, Form A
Pencil with eraser

Administration

Start
Grades K–1: Item 1
Grade 2: Item 4
Grades 3–4: Item 12
Grades 5–6: Item 22
Grade 7: Item 27
Grade 8: Item 32
Grades 9–12+: Item 37

Reverse
Grades 2–12+: Must pass the first 3 items administered, or reverse one item at a time until 3 consecutive scores of 1.

Discontinue
After 4 consecutive scores of 0.

Recording

Don’t Know (DK): Used to record when the examinee provides an answer of “I don’t know” as his or her only answer.

No Response (NR): Used to record when the examinee does not respond.

Repeated Item (R): Used to record when the examinee asks for an item to be repeated or when it appears that he or she was not attending.

Scoring

Score 0 or 1 point.

Score on the basis of spelling only. Do not penalize for:

  • poorly formed letters
  • use of upper- and/or lowercase letters
  • use of print and/or cursive

  • Each letter in the word must be recognizable and in the proper order to receive credit.
  • For Items 1–6, letter reversals are not penalized unless another letter is formed.
  • For Items 7–69, any response that includes a letter reversal, whether the reversal forms another letter or not, is scored 0.
  • On Item 31 (don’t) and Item 41 (she’s), score 0 if the examinee omits the apostrophe.

Refer to the KTEA–3 Administration and Scoring Manuals for additional information.
