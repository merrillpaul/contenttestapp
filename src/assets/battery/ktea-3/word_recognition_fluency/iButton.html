Materials

Client Device (Raised)

Administration

Prior to administration, listen to the audio files for Word Recognition Fluency (reference use only). Audio files are stored in Q-i Central under the Support tab (select Test Materials).

Start
Grades 1–2: Set A, Trial 1
Grades 3–12+: Set B, Trial 1

Reverse
Grades 3–12+: If the raw score equals 2 or less after Set B, Trial 1, drop back to Set A, Trial 1.

Discontinue
Grades 1–2: Discontinue after Set A if the combined raw score for Trials 1 and 2 is less than 48 (maximum).

Timing

Allow exactly 15 seconds per trial for the examinee to read as many words as possible.

Recording

Don’t Know (DK): Used to record when the examinee provides an answer of “I don’t know” as his or her only answer.

Scoring

Each word is worth 1 point. Any word not marked as an Uncorrected Error before the 15 second marker will be counted as correct. 

For credit, the examinee must say the word as a connected, relatively smooth whole, with the accent on the proper syllable.

Do not count self-corrections or repetitions as errors.

Uncorrected Error: Occurs when the examinee provides an incorrect response or skips a word, and does not correct the incorrect or skipped response. 

Self-correction: Occurs when the examinee provides an incorrect response or skips a word, but corrects the incorrect or skipped response.

Do not penalize for articulation differences due to dialect, regional speech patterns, or a first language other than English (e.g., an examinee whose first language is Spanish may pronounce the letter v as /b/).

Do not penalize for pronouncing tu as tyoo instead of too or choo (e.g., vituperate may be pronounced as vye TYOO pur ate).

Do not penalize for pronouncing nu as nyoo instead of noo (e.g., nugatory may be pronounced as NYOO guh tor ee).

Do not penalize for the American dialectal variation of pronouncing /t/ as /d/ in certain words, such as saying KUM fer duh bul for comfortable or MASS uh KISS dik for masochistic.

Refer to the KTEA–3 Administration Manual for additional information.
