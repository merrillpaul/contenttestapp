Examinee asks to take notes
Do not allow the examinee to take notes.
Examinee requests a repetition
Do not play a passage more than once, or answer any questions that the examinee asks about the story. However, you may repeat a test question at the examinee’s request or if you think he or she did not hear the question the first time. For multiple choice questions, you may repeat the list of choices a second time as needed.
