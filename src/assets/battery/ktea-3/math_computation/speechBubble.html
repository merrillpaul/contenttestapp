Instructions
Examinee asks to use a calculator
The examinee may not use a calculator.
Examinee does not attempt problems in order (left to right, row by row, left page first)
Redirect him or her.
Examinee seems easily distracted
If necessary, you may cover the opposite page in the Response Booklet to display only one page at a time.
Examinee has a severe motor impairment
Allow the examinee to respond orally.
