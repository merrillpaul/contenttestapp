Materials

Response Booklet, Form A
Pencil with eraser

Administration

Start
Grades K–2: Item 1
Grade 3: Item 12
Grades 4–12+: Item 24

Reverse
Grade 3: If the examinee scores 0 on any of the first 3 items administered, reverse one row of items at a time; administer items verbally as instructed. Repeat as necessary until 3 consecutive items are passed or Item 1 is reached.
Grades 4–12+: If the examinee scores 0 on any of the first 3 items administered, reverse one row of items at a time by saying, Now work these problems (point to the appropriate row of items). Repeat as necessary until 3 consecutive items are passed or Item 1 is reached. If the examinee reverses to Item 10 (or earlier), administer the item verbally as instructed.

Discontinue
After 4 consecutive scores of 0.

Recording

Don’t Know (DK): Used to record when the examinee provides an answer of “I don’t know” as his or her only answer.

No Response (NR): Used to record when the examinee does not respond.

Self-Correct (SC): Used to record when the examinee self-corrects.

Scoring

All items: Score 0 or 1 point.
  • Do not penalize for reversal or numeral formation errors except where indicated (i.e., Items 6–9).
  • Responses need not be reduced to lowest terms unless specified.
  • Improper fractions are acceptable.
  • In general, any answer that is numerically equivalent to the correct answer is correct.
  • Transposition errors, such as writing 61 for 16, are penalized.
  • Items before the basal will be counted as correct regardless of the score entered.

Refer to the KTEA–3 Administration and Scoring Manuals for additional information.
