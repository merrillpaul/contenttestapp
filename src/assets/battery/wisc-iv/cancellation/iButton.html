Administration

Start
Ages 6-16: Sample Item, Practice Item, then Item 1

Discontinue
After 45 seconds for each item

Timing

Accurate timing is essential. Begin timing after saying the last word of the instructions. Stop timing when the child completes the item or 45 seconds have elapsed.

Recording

Prompt Administered (P): Used to record when any subtest-specific prompt is provided  

Scoring

Use the scoring template to score the child's responses.

Subtract Number Incorrect from Number Correct. Total raw scores less than 0 are scored as 0.

Refer to the WISC-IV Administration and Scoring Manual for additional information.
