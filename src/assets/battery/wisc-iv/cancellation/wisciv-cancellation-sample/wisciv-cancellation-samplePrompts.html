Place Response Booklet 2 in front of the child. Be sure the child sees only the sample and practice on the cover page. Direct the child’s attention to the animals at the top of the page. Point to the row of animals in a sweeping motion from left to right and say, Look at this row. They are all animals.

Point to the sample and say, Look at this row. It has both animals and other objects in it. I am going to draw a line through each animal. I am not going to draw a line through anything else (draw lines through both animals).
