Instructions

Child provides verbal and nonverbal responses that are contradictory (e.g., says one but holds up two fingers)
Say, What do you mean?
Child hears a different word and responds incorrectly 
Repeat the item, emphasizing the misheard word.
Child asks you to repeat an item. 
Each item may be repeated as often as necessary, but do not alter the wording in any way.
Child’s response is unclear or followed by a Q in the sample responses.
Say, What do you mean? or Tell me more about it.
