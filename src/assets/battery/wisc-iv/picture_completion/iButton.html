Materials

Client Device (Flat)

Administration

Start
Ages 6-8: Sample Item, then Item 1
Ages 9-11: Sample Item, then Item 5
Ages 12-16: Sample Item, then Item 10
Children suspected of intellectual deficiency should start on Item 1.

Reverse
Ages 9-16: Score of 0 on either of the first two items given, administer preceding items in reverse order until two consecutive perfect scores are obtained. 

Discontinue
After 6 consecutive scores of 0

Timing

Accurate timing is essential. Begin timing for each item after presenting the item and saying the last word of the instructions. Stop timing when the child responds or 20 seconds have elapsed. 

Recording

Don't Know (DK): Used to record when the child provides an answer of "I don't know" as his or her only answer

No Response (NR): Used to record when the child does not respond

Prompt Administered (P): Used to record when any subtest-specific prompt is provided

†Teaching: Used to access corrective feedback for 0 point responses on Items 1 and 2

Show Me: Used to access feedback for ambiguous or incomplete responses

*Query: Used to access a query for a specific sample response on Item 29 

Scoring

Score 0 or 1 point.

Refer to the WISC-IV Administration and Scoring Manual for additional information.
