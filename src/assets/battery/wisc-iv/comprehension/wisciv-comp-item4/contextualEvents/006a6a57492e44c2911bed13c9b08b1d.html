If the first response is clearly incorrect, do not query.

If the child's response reflects only one general idea, ask for a second response by saying, Tell me another thing to do if you see thick smoke coming from the window of your neighbor's house.

If the second response refers to the same general concept as the first response, ask again for an additional response by saying, Tell me some more (reasons that, advantages of, problems with...).
