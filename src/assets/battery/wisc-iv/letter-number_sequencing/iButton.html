Administration

Read each trial verbatim at the rate of one number or letter per second, dropping your voice slightly on the last number or letter in the sequence.

Start
Ages 6-7: Qualifying Items, Sample Item, then Item 1
Ages 8-16: Sample Item, then Item 1
Children suspected of intellectual deficiency should start on the Qualifying Items.

Discontinue
If child is unable to respond correctly to either Qualifying Item or after scores of 0 on all three trials of an item

Recording

Don't Know (DK): Used to record when the child provides an answer of "I don't know" as his or her only answer

No Response (NR): Used to record when the child does not respond

Prompt Administered (P): Used to record when any subtest-specific prompt is provided 

Repeated Item (R): Used to record when the child asks for an item to be repeated

*Numbers Not First, *Letters Out of Order, and *Numbers Out of Order: Used to access corrective feedback regarding the ordering of numbers and letters

Scoring

Score 0 or 1 point for each trial.

Refer to the WISC-IV Administration and Scoring Manual for additional information.
