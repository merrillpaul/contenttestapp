Place page 1 of Response Booklet 1 in front of the child. Point to the key at the top of the page and say, Here are some shapes with marks in them. Each shape has its own mark (point to each shape).

Point to the sample items and say, Down here are some empty shapes. You are going to fill them in like this.

Point to the first sample item (circle), and say, Watch me. Here is a circle. This circle up here (point to circle in key) has two lines going this way. So I put the two lines in the circle like this (draw two lines in circle).

Point to the second sample item (star) and say, Watch me again. Here is a star. This star up here (point to star in key) has one line going up and down. So I put a line going up and down in the star like this (draw line in star).

Hand the child a #2 pencil without an eraser and say, Now you do these (point to remaining sample items). Stop when you get to this line (point to line that separates sample items from test items).

Allow the child to work alone on the remaining sample items.
