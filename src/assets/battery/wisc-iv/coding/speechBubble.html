Instructions
A left-handed child partially blocks the key with his or her left hand during Sample Items
Stop the administration. Place an extra Response Booklet to the right of the child’s Response Booklet so the extra key is aligned with the one the child’s hand is blocking. Have the child complete the remaining sample items using the separate key.
Child makes a spontaneous correction  
Do not discourage unless the child does so repeatedly and it impedes performance.
Child asks what to do if he or she makes a mistake
Say, That’s OK. Just keep working as fast as you can.
Child omits an item or begins to complete a row in reverse order 
Say, Do them in order. Don’t skip any. Point to the first omitted item and say, Do this one next.
