Say to the child, Now I am going to show you some pictures. I will read something to you, and I want you to point to the picture that shows what I say.

 

Direct the child’s attention to the pictures by saying, Let’s look at each of these pictures. Look at this picture, and this picture, and this one, and this one. Point to pictures 1–4 in turn. Say, Show me the ball.

 

If the child points to the ball, say, Good, you looked at all of the pictures and pointed to the ball. Now let’s do some more. Listen carefully to what I say and then point to the picture that shows what I say. I can say it again if you need me to.
