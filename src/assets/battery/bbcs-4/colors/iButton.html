Client Device (Flat)

Administration

Start
Ages 2:6 - 7:11: Item 1

Recording

Don’t Know (DK): Used to record when the examinee provides an answer of “I don’t know” as his or her only answer.

No Response (NR): Used to record when the examinee does not respond.

Repeated Item (R): Used to record when the examinee asks for an item to be repeated or when it appears that they were not attending.

Scoring

Score 0 or 1 point.

Refer to the BBCS-4 Examiner's Manual for additional information.
