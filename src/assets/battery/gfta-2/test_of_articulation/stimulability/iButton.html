Materials

None

Administration

Start
Begin with the first sound/word position. 

Discontinue
If the examinee cannot repeat the target correctly after multiple stimulation, discontinue stimulability testing of that sound/word position. 

Recording

Correct on first stimulation
Touch target once.

Correct on multiple stimulation
Touch target twice.

Not stimulable
Touch target three times.

Not administered
Do not record any response.

Scoring

No scoring. Errors are recorded for qualitative analysis.

Refer to the GFTA-2 Manual for additional information.
