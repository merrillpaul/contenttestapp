Materials

Client Device (Raised)

Administration

Start
Younger Examinees: Story 1- A Bad Night for Jerry
Older Examinees: Story 2 - Jack and Rachel
(Both paragraphs may be administered.)

Discontinue
Do not discontinue. Administer all items within the story.

Recording

Substitutions
Select misarticulated phoneme from target word. Select substituted phoneme from IPA keyboard.

Omissions
Select omitted phoneme from target word. Select the dash symbol below IPA keyboard.

Distortions
Phoneme distortions requiring diacritical marks can be recorded in the verbatim handwriting capture area located next to each target word.

Additions
Phoneme additions to target words can be recorded in the verbatim handwriting capture area located next to each target word. 

Scoring

No scoring. Errors are recorded for qualitative analysis.

Refer to the GFTA-2 Manual for additional information.
